Complete Editorial Strategy Methodology

Strategic Framework

Comprehensive topical authority development through systematic content creation targeting identified authority gaps in high-value market segments.

Authority Gap Analysis Process

  1. Competitive Content Audit: Analysis of top 10 competitors' content coverage
  2. Keyword Gap Identification: SEMRush and Ahrefs analysis of uncovered high-intent terms
  3. Topic Cluster Mapping: Grouping related keywords into coherent content themes
  4. Authority Scoring: Quantifying current vs potential authority in each cluster

Content Strategy Development

  • Pillar Content Creation: Comprehensive guides for each topic cluster
  • Supporting Content Network: Related articles linking to pillar content
  • Internal Linking Strategy: Strategic link architecture to build topical authority
  • Content Optimization: E-A-T signals and semantic SEO implementation

Revenue Attribution Model

Revenue calculations based on:

  • Historical conversion data from similar content initiatives
  • Industry benchmarks for B2B SaaS content performance
  • Conservative traffic projections based on keyword search volumes
  • Authority content conversion premium (47% higher than standard content)

Implementation Timeline

  • Months 1-2: Content strategy finalization and pillar content creation
  • Months 3-6: Supporting content development and internal linking
  • Months 7-12: Content optimization and authority building

Success Metrics

  • Topical authority scores (Koray.io methodology)
  • Organic traffic growth in target clusters
  • Keyword ranking improvements
  • Content-driven lead generation and revenue attribution
